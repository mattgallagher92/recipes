# Cheese sauce

## Ingredients

- 500ml soya milk
- 3 tbsp plain flour
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp nutritional yeast

## Cooking

- Heat the olive oil in a saucepan
- Stir in the flour
- Add the milk
- Heat, stirring with a whisk until thickened
- Take off the heat and stir through the nutritional yeast
