# Bean stew with dumplings

Serves 2.

## Ingredients

For the stew:

- 120g dried cannellini beans
- 1.5 onions
- 1 large carrot
- ½ leek
- 2 sticks celery
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 2 tbsps flour
- ½ tbsp tomato purée
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 250ml red wine
- 500ml stock
- ½ tsp Marmite
- 1 bay leaf
- Oil

For the dumplings:

- 75g vegetable suet
- 150g self-raising flour

## Preparation

- Soak the cannellini beans overnight
- Rinse the cannellini beans
- Chop the onions, carrot, leek and celery into chunks of roughly 1cm
- Crush the garlic

## Cooking

- Boil the beans for 10 minutes; set aside
- Heat oil in a pan
- Add the onions; fry for 2 minutes
- Add the garlic; fry for 1 minute
- Add the carrot; fry for 7 minutes
- Add the flour; stir
- Add purée; stir
- Add the balsamic vinegar; stir
- Add the red wine, stock and Marmite; stir
- Add the beans, leek, celery and bay leaf; simmer for 90 minutes
- Preheat the oven to 180°C
- Mix the suet and flour with water, according to the instructions on the suet packet; roll into balls
- Transfer the stew to a casserole dish; put the dumplings on top
- Top with foil; bake for 20 minutes
- Remove foil; bake for 20 more minutes
